Protection Tips

  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Check URLs before clicking
  • Look for https:// in websites
  • Be wary of urgent messages
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep software updated

If You're a Victim

  1. Call 1930 immediately
  2. Report at cybercrime.gov.in
  3. Contact your bank
  4. Change all passwords
  5. Save evidence
